My reordering strategy focuses on maximizing leaf factoring by placing crate-only predicates (`clear`, `on`) first and the highly-connected `at` predicate last in the predicates block, ordering actions so that Drive (truck-only) comes first followed by single-subsystem-leaning actions, and within each action placing crate-local preconditions/effects before hoist/place/truck references to help DecStar's SCC analysis strip crates into leaves more efficiently. Additionally, I reorder Load and Unload preconditions to emphasize the crate's local state (`in`, `lifting`) before shared location predicates, which should help the interaction graph analysis minimize cross-leaf edges and potentially resolve the instance-12 timeout.

(define (domain Depot)
(:requirements :strips :typing)
(:types place locatable - object
	depot distributor - place
        truck hoist surface - locatable
        pallet crate - surface)

(:predicates (clear ?x - surface)
             (on ?x - crate ?y - surface)
             (in ?x - crate ?y - truck)
             (lifting ?x - hoist ?y - crate)
             (available ?x - hoist)
             (at ?x - locatable ?y - place))

(:action Drive
:parameters (?x - truck ?y - place ?z - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?y))
:effect (and (not (at ?x ?y)) (at ?x ?z)))

(:action Lift
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (on ?y ?z) (clear ?y) (at ?y ?p) (available ?x) (at ?x ?p))
:effect (and (not (on ?y ?z)) (not (clear ?y)) (clear ?z) (not (at ?y ?p)) (lifting ?x ?y) (not (available ?x))))

(:action Drop
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (clear ?z) (lifting ?x ?y) (at ?z ?p) (at ?x ?p))
:effect (and (on ?y ?z) (clear ?y) (at ?y ?p) (not (clear ?z)) (not (lifting ?x ?y)) (available ?x)))

(:action Load
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (lifting ?x ?y) (at ?x ?p) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (in ?y ?z) (not (lifting ?x ?y)) (available ?x)))

(:action Unload
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (in ?y ?z) (available ?x) (at ?x ?p) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (lifting ?x ?y) (not (in ?y ?z)) (not (available ?x))))
