My reordering strategy returns to the successful Stage 2 approach by placing goal predicates (`on`, `clear`, `at`) first with mutual-exclusion-friendly grouping, but critically reorders actions differently from Iteration 1: `Lift` and `Drop` first (the core goal-achieving pair for `on`/`clear`), then `Unload`/`Load` (supporting truck operations), and finally `Drive`, since the backward-chaining heuristic benefits from having the direct `on`-achieving `Drop` and its immediate precondition-provider `Lift` with the lowest action variable IDs. Within each action, I reorder preconditions so static/easily-satisfied conditions (like `at ?x ?p` for hoists which rarely change) come first to maximize unit propagation, and place add-effects before delete-effects with goal-relevant effects first.

(define (domain Depot)
(:requirements :strips :typing)
(:types place locatable - object
	depot distributor - place
        truck hoist surface - locatable
        pallet crate - surface)

(:predicates (on ?x - crate ?y - surface)
             (clear ?x - surface)
             (at ?x - locatable ?y - place)
             (in ?x - crate ?y - truck)
             (available ?x - hoist)
             (lifting ?x - hoist ?y - crate))

(:action Lift
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?p) (available ?x) (at ?y ?p) (on ?y ?z) (clear ?y))
:effect (and (lifting ?x ?y) (clear ?z) (not (at ?y ?p)) (not (clear ?y)) (not (available ?x)) (not (on ?y ?z))))

(:action Drop
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?p) (lifting ?x ?y) (at ?z ?p) (clear ?z))
:effect (and (on ?y ?z) (at ?y ?p) (clear ?y) (available ?x) (not (lifting ?x ?y)) (not (clear ?z))))

(:action Unload
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?p) (available ?x) (at ?z ?p) (in ?y ?z))
:effect (and (lifting ?x ?y) (not (in ?y ?z)) (not (available ?x))))

(:action Load
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?p) (lifting ?x ?y) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (in ?y ?z) (available ?x) (not (lifting ?x ?y))))

(:action Drive
:parameters (?x - truck ?y - place ?z - place)
:precondition (and (at ?x ?y))
:effect (and (at ?x ?z) (not (at ?x ?y))))
