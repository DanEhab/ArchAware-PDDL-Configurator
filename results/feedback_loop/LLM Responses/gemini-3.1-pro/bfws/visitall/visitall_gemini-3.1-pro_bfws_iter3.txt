To optimize Engine 2's (Fast Downward) invariant synthesis and grounding, the static predicate `connected` is moved to the first position in the action's precondition. In the predicate declarations, `visited` is kept first to ensure it receives the lowest internal ID for novelty prioritization, while `connected` is placed before `at-robot` to group static structural elements early in the parsing pipeline.

```lisp
(define (domain grid-visit-all)
(:requirements :typing)
(:types        place - object)
(:predicates (visited ?x - place)
	     (connected ?x ?y - place)
	     (at-robot ?x - place)
)
	
(:action move
:parameters (?curpos ?nextpos - place)
:precondition (and (connected ?curpos ?nextpos) (at-robot ?curpos))
:effect (and (visited ?nextpos) (at-robot ?nextpos) (not (at-robot ?curpos)))
)

)
```
