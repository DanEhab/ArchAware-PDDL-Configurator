I prioritized the `visited` predicate and its corresponding add-effect over `at-robot` because `visited` directly represents the primary task completion goal in this domain. This adjustment accelerates landmark acceptance tracking and focuses the FF heuristic's forward propagation on achieving the unvisited waypoints, further optimizing search guidance.

```lisp
(define (domain grid-visit-all)
(:requirements :typing)
(:types        place - object)
(:predicates (visited ?x - place)
	     (at-robot ?x - place)
	     (connected ?x ?y - place)
)
	
(:action move
:parameters (?curpos ?nextpos - place)
:precondition (and (connected ?curpos ?nextpos) (at-robot ?curpos))
:effect (and (visited ?nextpos) (at-robot ?nextpos) (not (at-robot ?curpos)))
)

)
```
