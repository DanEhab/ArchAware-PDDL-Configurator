I maintained `visited` as the first add-effect to ensure the FF heuristic's forward propagation remains focused on the primary goal, while strictly adhering to the static-first precondition rule.

(:requirements :typing)
(:types        place - object)
(:predicates (at-robot ?x - place)
	     (visited ?x - place)

:parameters (?curpos ?nextpos - place)
:precondition (and (connected ?curpos ?nextpos) (at-robot ?curpos))
:effect (and (visited ?nextpos) (at-robot ?nextpos) (not (at-robot ?curpos)))
