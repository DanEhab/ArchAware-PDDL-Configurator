I reordered the domain to prioritize goal-facing placement structure by putting the placement predicates first and ordering actions so direct achievers of typical Depot goals appear before support and transport actions, while preserving every literal exactly as given. I kept each action’s preconditions and effects semantically identical and only changed their order conservatively to bias BFWS-Preference’s internal IDs and relaxed-plan tie-breaking toward stack-construction fluents without repeating the prior invalid semantic edits.
(define (domain Depot)
(:requirements :strips :typing)
(:types place locatable - object
	depot distributor - place
        truck hoist surface - locatable
        pallet crate - surface)

(:predicates (on ?x - crate ?y - surface)
             (at ?x - locatable ?y - place) 
             (clear ?x - surface)
             (in ?x - crate ?y - truck)
             (lifting ?x - hoist ?y - crate)
             (available ?x - hoist))
	
(:action Drop 
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (lifting ?x ?y) (clear ?z) (at ?x ?p) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (at ?y ?p) (clear ?y) (on ?y ?z) (available ?x) (not (lifting ?x ?y)) (not (clear ?z))))

(:action Lift
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - surface ?p - place)
:precondition (and (at ?y ?p) (on ?y ?z) (clear ?y) (at ?x ?p) (available ?x))
:effect (and (lifting ?x ?y) (clear ?z) (not (at ?y ?p)) (not (clear ?y)) (not (available ?x)) 
             (not (on ?y ?z))))

(:action Unload 
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (in ?y ?z) (available ?x) (at ?x ?p) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (lifting ?x ?y) (not (in ?y ?z)) (not (available ?x))))

(:action Load
:parameters (?x - hoist ?y - crate ?z - truck ?p - place)
:precondition (and (lifting ?x ?y) (at ?x ?p) (at ?z ?p))
:effect (and (in ?y ?z) (available ?x) (not (lifting ?x ?y))))

(:action Drive
:parameters (?x - truck ?y - place ?z - place) 
:precondition (and (at ?x ?y))
:effect (and (at ?x ?z) (not (at ?x ?y)))))
